📘 How RentoVerse Works

  1. Request — Renters browse rooms and send a request. Status starts at PENDING_REQUEST.
  2. Decision — Providers approve or decline. Approval moves the booking to AWAITING_PAYMENT.
  3. Deposit 25% — Renter pays within 3 days (paymentDeadline). After admin verification, the booking becomes CONFIRMED.
  4. Visit & Decide — Within the viewing window, renter either Confirm Completion or Request Refund.
  5. Finalize — Admin completes or refunds. Once completed, the provider can request their 25% payout.

💳 Payment (bKash · Nagad · Rocket)

  • Deposit 25% — Pay securely via bKash, Nagad or Rocket. Admin verifies the deposit and the booking moves to CONFIRMED.
  • Viewing & Decision — Visit the room, then choose Confirm Completion or Request Refund.
  • Provider Payout — After completion, the provider requests their 25% payout. Admin processes and notifies.
RentoVerse is a trusted, professional platform—payments are secure and auditable.
